üì± What is the Hungr Merchant Wallet?

The Hungr Merchant Wallet is a digital wallet inside the Hungr Merchant App. Merchants can:

  • Add money (Top-Up) - Deposit funds to use for Hungr services
  • Withdraw money - Transfer wallet balance to their bank or e-wallet
  • View history - See all their transactions

üíµ How Merchants Deposit Money (Top-Up)

1

Merchant sends money to Hungr

They deposit to our bank account OR send via GCash/Maya. They keep the reference number and take a screenshot.

2

Merchant submits request in the app

In the Merchant App ‚Üí Wallet ‚Üí Top Up, they enter the amount, payment method, reference number, and upload the screenshot as proof.

3

Admin verifies and approves

You (admin) check if the payment actually arrived in our account. Look at the proof screenshot, verify the amount matches, then click Approve.

4

Merchant wallet is credited

Once approved, the money is instantly added to their wallet balance. They get a notification in their app.

üí∏ How Merchants Withdraw Money

1

Merchant must enable 2FA first

For security, merchants need to set up Google Authenticator before they can withdraw. This is a one-time setup.

2

Merchant submits withdrawal request

In the app ‚Üí Wallet ‚Üí Withdraw, they enter the amount, destination (GCash/Maya/Bank), account number, and the 6-digit code from their authenticator app.

3

Admin processes the payout

Manual payout is done by finance team. The system records the request and the merchant is notified when complete.

üîí Security Features (Why Our Wallet is Secure)

üìí

Unchangeable Transaction History

Once money is added or removed, no one can edit or delete the record. The database has special locks that prevent this.

üîó

Chain of Records

Every transaction is linked to the previous one using special codes. If anyone tries to change old records, the system will know.

üîë

Two Keys Required

To approve a top-up, you need BOTH your login AND the System Key. Even if someone steals your password, they can't approve without the key.

üì±

Google Authenticator for Withdrawals

Merchants must enter a code from their phone every time they withdraw. The code changes every 30 seconds.

üö´

No Duplicate Transactions

If a merchant accidentally clicks "Submit" twice, the system will only process it once. No double charges!

üìã

Everything is Logged

Every action (who approved what, when, from where) is recorded. This helps us track any suspicious activity.

‚úÖ How to Approve a Top-Up Request

  1. Check our bank account or GCash for the incoming payment
  2. Match the amount - does the deposit match what they're requesting?
  3. Match the reference number - is it the same as in our account?
  4. Look at the proof screenshot - click to enlarge and verify
  5. If everything matches, click Approve
  6. Add notes (optional) like "Verified in BDO 12:30pm"

‚ùå When to Reject a Request

Reject a request if:

  • The payment did not arrive in our account
  • The amount in the proof doesn't match the request
  • The screenshot looks fake or edited
  • The reference number is already used in a previous request
  • The payment is too old (more than 7 days)

Always write a clear reason so the merchant understands why it was rejected.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I undo an approval?

A: No. Once approved, the transaction is permanent. This is a security feature. If there was an error, contact the tech team.

Q: What if I can't see the proof image?

A: The image might not have uploaded correctly. Ask the merchant to submit a new request with a clearer screenshot.

Q: How long should approval take?

A: Try to approve within 24 hours. Merchants are waiting for their balance to be credited.

Q: What's the System Key?

A: It's a secret password shared only with authorized admins. It's required to approve transactions. Never share it with anyone outside the admin team.

ü™ô Hungr Coins Guide

Everything you need to know about the Hungr Coins reward system

ü™ô What are Hungr Coins?

Hungr Coins are a reward currency that can be given to customers as incentives. Unlike the wallet (which uses real pesos), coins are:

  • Bought by Merchants & Riders - They pay pesos to get coins
  • Awarded to Customers - As rewards for loyalty, orders, or feedback
  • Redeemable - Customers can use coins for discounts
Key Difference from Wallet: Wallet = Real money. Coins = Reward points.

üí∞ How the Coin System Works

1

Merchants/Riders buy coins from Hungr

They pay us (via GCash, Maya, or bank), then submit a purchase request with proof of payment. Same process as wallet top-up!

2

Admin approves the purchase

You verify the payment arrived, then approve. Their coin balance is credited instantly.

3

They award coins to customers

Merchants can award coins to any customer by email (for repeat customers, promotions, etc.). Riders award coins automatically when a customer gives them 5-star feedback.

4

Customers earn and spend coins

Customers see their coin balance in the Hungr app. They can also earn coins from the system for completing orders, bulk purchases, etc.

The system automatically awards coins for these actions (configurable in database):

‚úÖ How to Approve Coin Purchase Requests

  1. Click the ü™ô Coin Requests tab at the top
  2. Choose Merchant Coins or Rider Coins toggle
  3. Check our bank/GCash for the incoming payment
  4. Match the peso amount - does it match what they paid?
  5. Verify the proof screenshot
  6. If valid, click Approve - coins are instantly credited!
Note: Coin purchases require the System Key, just like wallet approvals.

‚ùì Coins FAQ

Q: What's the exchange rate for coins?

A: The merchant/rider pays in pesos, and admin sets how many coins they get. It's flexible - you could do ‚Ç±1 = 1 coin, or offer bulk discounts.

Q: Can merchants withdraw coins back to pesos?

A: No. Once pesos are converted to coins, they can only be awarded to customers. Coins are a one-way reward currency.

Q: What happens if a merchant has no coins but tries to award?

A: The system prevents it. They'll see an "Insufficient coins" error and must buy more first.

Q: Can we add/remove earning rules later?

A: Yes! The earning rules are stored in the database (coin_earning_rules table) and can be modified. Just set is_active = FALSE to disable a rule.
